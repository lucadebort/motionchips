What do we mean by production stage?
Whenever we reach a content milestone, we'll send it to you to get your feedback.
Based on the chosen package, you'll have a certain number of opportunities for special requests and edits.

What production stages do we tackle?
Scripting the video and the text to be handled by the narrator, choosing the art style, drafting the visual storyboard and creating the animation.

How we work

1

Getting in touch and proposal

You: Reach out via the contact form below, tell us about the video you have in mind and the package you'd like to go with.

Us: We'll get back to you ASAP with a custom proposal. With your approval, we're ready to go!

2

Planning

You: With the proposal accepted, you just need to send us any logos, assets, fonts or visual guidelines to use.

Us: We'll send you some questions to help us with the copy and we'll get to writing.

3

Production and Delivery

You: Sit back and relax! All you need to do is give us feedback on the drafts and concepts we send you (based on your selected package).

Us: We'll work on the video (copy, graphics, music, narration) and send you the storyboards for approval. Then we'll start working our animation magic, followed up by another round of feedback. When all's said and done, we'll deliver the finalized video. Now that's what we call poetry in motion.
